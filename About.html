About us:



Scheduling an event and the permission process behind it becomes a hectic work for the organizing committees.
Happenings is an Advanced Event Scheduler Website where college students get to schedule or plan an event.
It makes it easier for the committee members to check the availability of dates and time. Students also get to track
the upcoming events taking place in the college. Using Happenings makes registration of events easier for students.
